Edwyna Help & Support

Log in to your Edwyna dashboard, go to Profile, and click “Edit”. You can change your name,contact number.

Browse events from the homepage or events page. Click “Register”, complete required info and payment (if any). You can track status under “My Events”.

Go to the Certificates section. You’ll find all earned certificates with download links in PDF format.

Results are published on the Results page. You can view leaderboards, performance stats, and download result sheets.

Your referral link is found on the Referral page. Share it with peers. You earn rewards each time a new user registers and participates via your Referral Code.
